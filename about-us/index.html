About us

Heritage counselling and consultancy center is a registered training institution accredited by TVET as a training and assessment center in accordance with sections 18,32 and 57 of TVET Act 2013.

We are also registered by the kenya counselling and psychological association (KCPA) to train, examine and assess students studying counselling and psychology at the levels of certificate and diploma.

We offer diverse psychological therapies and consultancy services

Our main focus in HCCC is on individuals, groups organizations, institutions of higher learning, Community Based Organization and business entities.

Our Vision

To enhance good mental health as a corner-stone for personal, academic and career success.

Our Mission

Dedicated to provide high quality mental health services through counselling and training that supports individual's ability to benefit from their experiences.

Our Objectives

  • To support an individual's wellness within the staff of an organization to increase productivity and to understand and accept themselves "AS THEY ARE".
  • To streamline quality training, practice and supervision of Counselling and Psychological services
  • To provide confidential services within the bounds of legal and ethical frameworks.
  • To enhance team work and interpersonal relationships through capacity building.
  • To provide a safe haven for clients to freely face their fears in enhancing their decision-making abilities.
  • To impart knowledge, skills and techniques on counselling through training and research in counselling and psychology
  • To provide linkages with other National and international professional Associations.

Our Core Values

  • Client first
  • Results-oriented
  • Encompassing accountability
  • Teamwork
  • Spirituality
  • Congruency

Our Guarantee

Heritage Counselling and Consultancy services exemplifies the highest standards of counselling and training as guided by both the TVET and KCPA regulations.

We are dedicated to providing quality care and service to our clients while upholding all ethical standards as stipulated in the KCPA Code of regulations.
