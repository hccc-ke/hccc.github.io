HCCC Training

Heritage counselling and consultancy center is a registered training institution accredited by TVET as a training and assessment center in accordance with sections 18,32 and 57 of TVET Act 2013.

We are also registered by the kenya counselling and psychological association (KCPA) to train, examine and assess students studying counselling and psychology at the levels of certificate and diploma.

Accredited courses Offered

  • Basic counselling skills
  • Crisis Counselling
  • Behavior change communication
  • Psychological debriefing trauma counselling.
  • Marriage and family therapy
  • Marital counselling.
  • Premarital counselling.
  • Work-place counselling.
  • Loss, Grief and bereavement counselling.
  • Dealing with depression in the family.
  • Drugs and substance abuse.
  • Effective parenting skills.
  • Career counselling.
  • Personal therapy.
  • Child and adolescents counselling.
  • Addiction counselling
  • HIV and AIDS counselling.
  • Special needs and disabilities counselling.
